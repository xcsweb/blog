All want to be fully satisfied but are unaware
of the calamity afterwards.

志得意滿人皆願，不知凶險藏其間。

Don’t you know how many people have fallen from the
pinnacle of their success, which happens from time to time beside me？ 

多少功成身遂殞，朝夕猝發於身邊。
