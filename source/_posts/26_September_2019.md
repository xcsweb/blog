Now without the heat and mosquitoes, I can have
a good sleep at the long autumn night.

秋夜漫漫恰得眠，燥熱去兮蚊不見。  

I’m lying comfortably on the warm kang feeling
no cold. The sun also seems tired and rises late.

寒逺我兮身下暖，朝陽遲遲亦困倦。
