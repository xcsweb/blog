The swarms of mosquitoes were bearing down
menacingly and encircling me in countless tight rings. 

蚊軍攻勢一何洶，圍我四萬八千重。They kept buzzing around me up to midnight
in an attempt to suck all my blood and flesh. 

欲將血肉吮吸凈，嗡嗡不去至三更。
