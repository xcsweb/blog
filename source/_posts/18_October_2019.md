He’s a hard drinker that can drain the rivers.
He smokes as a chimney making suffocating clouds. 

豪飮吸百川，勁嘬吐雲煙。

It has got round that he has been stricken with
epilepsy. That is why he has been absent recently.   

近日忽不見，傳言患癲癇。
