For fear of being accused of giving others what
he deserted, he left it on the roadside. 

己不欲兮棄路旁，倘施人兮恐遭謗。

The poor passer⁃by was overjoyed to come across
it and wished lasting happiness to the former owner. 

貧者遇兮喜洋洋，祝彼君兮福無疆。
