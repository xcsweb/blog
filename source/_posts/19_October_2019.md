installation began pouring smoke into the air yesterday. But the pipes still
felt cool in our apartment building. 

供暖揚高煙，樓内猶凉管。Thanks to the mercy of Heaven, the arrival of the cold air
has been postponed. 

所幸見天憐，寒意故姗姗。
